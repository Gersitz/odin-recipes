Lasagna

Tasty Lasagna Bolognese

This is a very straight forward recipe using ingredients you most likely already have at home.

Ingredients

  • 500g minced beef
  • 500g minced pork
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 celery stick
  • 0.5 brown onion
  • 2 bottles italian passata
  • 1 glass red wine
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Provolone cheese
  • Buffalo Mozzarella
  • Grated Pecorino
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Extra virgin olive oil

    • For Béchamel Sauce:

  • 25 grams butter
  • 25 grams plain flour
  • 250 ml milk
  • sprinkle of salt
  • sprinkle of nutmeg

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the carrot, celery and onion and add a generous amount of extra virgin olive oil to a large pot.
  2. Add the chopped carrot, celery and onion to the pot and leave it to cook for 5-10 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  3. Add the minced meat to the pot. Break it down with a wooden spoon while it cooks. Leave it to simmer and stir occasionally.
  4. Add 1.5 bottles of passata and stir. The stove should be on a medium-low heat. Cover the pot and let it simmer for one hour. Stir occasionally.
  5. Remove the lid after an hour and add one glass of water. Add more water if it looks too dry. Let it cook for one more hour without the lid.
  6. For the béchamel, put a small saucepan on your stove at low heat and add the butter.
  7. Let the butter melt, then slowly add the flour and stir with a whisk until the flour has disappeared. Add the milk in small steps while stirring continously.
  8. After the sauce is creamy and thick, turn off the heat and add a sprinkle of salt and nutmeg.
  9. To assemble the lasagna: Take a baking tray and coat it with extra virgin olive oil or butter.
  10. Add a scoop of the lasagna sauce at the bottom of the tray and spread it. Sprinkle pecorino/parmigiano cheese on top, then add provolone and fresh mozzarella.
  11. Add some béchamel - not too much. Add a layer of lasagna sheets. Put more layers in the same order until you get the amount of layers you want - you can try to aim for 5-6 layers.
  12. Add the rest of your italian passata on top of the last lasagna sheets.
  13. Cover the lasagna with aluminum foil and place it in a pre-heated oven for 20 minutes at 180°C/350°F.
  14. Remove from the oven and remove the foil. Add a generous sprinkle of pecorino/parmigiano cheese.
  15. Bake the lasagna again with no foil for another 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and let it cool.
